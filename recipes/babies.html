The World's Most Perfect Babies

What Are They??

Probably the greatest treat your greasy fists will ever clutch. Here's what you'll need.

Ingredients

  • sausage
  • bread
  • apple
  • cheese

How to make it right!

  1. Cut up them apples into dice-sized cubes. Put them in a pot with some water and sugar and start boiling.
  2. Fry up the sausage in burger-sized patties.
  3. In the remaining sausage grease, fry two sliced of bread with some cheese on one of them. Once cooked to light golden crispiness, put them together as a grilled cheese sandwich.
  4. Hopefully by this point the apples are soft because its time to put it all together. Put the sandwich on a plate, put the cooked sausage patty on top, then a big scoop of apples on top of that.
  5. Guess what? It's time to eat that thing. It's called a baby makes you feel warm and safe like a baby.
