The World's Most Perfect Shrimp Pasta

What is it??

Ain't nothin wrong with some good ole Shrimp Pasta from daddy's kitchen. This is all completely made up.

Ingredients

  • Gulf Shrimp
  • Cheese (orange is preferable)
  • Wild Spaghetti (6 oz)
  • Butter

How to make it right!

  1. Slice the shrimp eastways and let soften for 2 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, peel the spaghetti until wet. Bring to a rolling boil.
  3. Bring the butter to a near burn, and add Shrimp and yellow cheese.
  4. When pasta is halfway to al dente, add to shrimp-cheese mixture.
  5. Stir and simmer until pasta and shrimp are married and complete. Dig IN!
